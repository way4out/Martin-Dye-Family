### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol (Shelf-Stable & Non-Toxic)

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 58–78%  
**Purity**: ≥99% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion, and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Super-Donor Core (Biocompatible Foundation)
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)
- Advanced PEG-amine mix (2.4 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant precursor (0.7 eq) + cyclopentadiene precursor (1.1 eq)
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination using Pd-PEPPSI-IPr catalyst (2.5 mol%), NaOtBu (3.6 eq), dry toluene, 108–118°C, 18–22 h
- Workup: Celite filtration, flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc), recrystallization from ethanol
- **Yield**: 76–85%

#### Step 2: Adaptive Long-Chain Bridge Precursor
- Cool core to –78°C in dry THF
- Add t-BuLi (2.3 eq) dropwise, stir 110–140 min
- Add rigid dodecamethine+ linker with embedded thiophene spacers and stabilizing groups (1.06 eq)
- Quench with degassed NH₄Cl, extract DCM, recrystallize THF/hexane
- **Yield**: 70–79%

#### Step 3: Final Adaptive Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + dual BHT-type scavengers (6 mol%)
- Heat 72–88°C for 3.5–5.5 h under dark conditions
- Cool rapidly, triple precipitation with cold MTBE
- **Yield**: 65–76%

#### Step 4: Sulfonation, Shielding & Crystallization (Non-Toxic Finish)
- 1,3-propane sultone (4 eq) in dry DMF, 58–68°C, 4–5 h
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form
- Final purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid)
- Lyophilization + multiple recrystallizations from ethanol/water to produce highly stable, low-toxicity crystalline powder
- **Recovery**: 84–91%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Final Preparation
- ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC ≥99%
- UV-Vis-NIR (confirm >99.7% visible transmittance + broad SWIR response)
- Photostability and biocompatibility testing
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE container at room temperature with desiccant. Highly stable.
