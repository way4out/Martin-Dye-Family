### Overview
AetherVoid-5S is the **ultimate shelf-stable flagship** of the Martin Dye Family. It combines the optically void (>99% visible transmittance) performance of AetherVoid-5 with enhanced antioxidant shielding, steric protection, and a highly crystalline zwitterionic form — allowing convenient room-temperature storage while delivering record-breaking ultra-broad SWIR absorption and near-infinite photostability.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Optically void / water-clear (>99% transmittance 400–700 nm)
- **Absorption Range**: Ultra-broad SWIR (900–2200+ nm) with strong dual peaks ~950 nm and ~1650 nm + massive tail
- **Photostability**: Near-infinite (advanced antiaromatic reversal + radical-scavenging fused cage + built-in hindered phenols)
- **Solubility**: Universal — excellent in water, ethanol, DMSO, lipids, and polymer matrices
- **Molecular Formula (proposed)**: C₆₅H₈₂N₄O₁₄S₃Na₃ (shielded super-donor version)
- **Purity Target**: ≥99% (optical grade)
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE bottle at **room temperature (15–25°C)** for 12+ months. Refrigeration optional for 2+ years.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 60–80%  
**Purity**: ≥99% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Super-Donor Core Assembly
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)  
- PEG-amine mix (2.3 eq) + cyclopentadiene precursor (1.1 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant (0.6 eq)  
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2.5 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 16–20 h  
- Workup: Celite filtration, concentrate  
- Purification: Flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc) + recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 78–86%

#### Step 2: Stabilized Dodecamethine Bridge Precursor
- Cool shielded core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi (2.3 eq) dropwise, stir 100–130 min  
- Add rigid dodecamethine linker with embedded thiophene spacers (1.05 eq)  
- Warm slowly to –40°C, quench with degassed saturated NH₄Cl  
- Extract with DCM, dry over MgSO₄  
- Purification: Recrystallization from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 72–80%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Polymethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + extra BHT-type radical scavenger (6 mol%)  
- Heat at 75–90°C for 3–5 h under dark conditions (monitor NIR shift)  
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE or diethyl ether  
- **Yield**: 68–78%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation & Crystallization
- React with 1,3-propane sultone (4 eq) in dry DMF at 60–70°C for 4–5 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid gradient)  
- Lyophilization followed by final recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain highly stable crystalline powder  
- **Recovery**: 85–92%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Storage
- **Analytical Data**: ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC purity ≥99%  
- UV-Vis-NIR spectrum (confirm flat visible baseline and ultra-broad SWIR peaks)  
- Photostability test under continuous NIR irradiation  
- **Storage Recommendation**: Amber vial or HDPE bottle at room temperature with silica gel desiccant packet. Highly resistant to air, light, and moderate humidity.
