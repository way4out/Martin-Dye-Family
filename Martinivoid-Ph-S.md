### Overview
Martinivoid-Ph-S is the **room-temperature shelf-stable** version of Martinivoid-Ph (MD-003-Ph). It is optimized for deep NIR-IIb/III photoacoustic applications with enhanced radical shielding and a highly crystalline form, allowing easy storage without deep freezing while preserving powerful photoacoustic signal and near-transparent visible properties.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Visually near-transparent / pale in solution
- **Absorption Range**: Deep NIR-IIb/III (1350–1550+ nm) with strong tail beyond 1700 nm
- **Photostability**: Excellent to near-infinite (rigidified undecamethine structure + iodine enhancement + built-in antioxidants)
- **Solubility**: Excellent in water, ethanol, DMSO, and biological matrices (sulfonated/PEG form)
- **Molecular Formula (proposed)**: C₅₂H₆₄Cl₂IN₃O₉S₃Na₃ (iodine-optimized shielded version)
- **Purity Target**: ≥98% (optical grade)
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE bottle at **room temperature (15–25°C)** for 12+ months. Refrigeration for 2+ years.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 60–78%  
**Purity**: ≥98% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Undecamethine Core Assembly
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)  
- Secondary amines (2.2 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant precursor (0.6 eq)  
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2.5 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 18–22 h  
- Workup: Celite filtration, concentrate  
- Purification: Flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc) + recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 72–82%

#### Step 2: Organolithium Iodine-Optimized Bridge Precursor
- Cool shielded core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi (2.3 eq) dropwise, stir 100–130 min  
- Add rigid undecamethine linker with iodine substitution and thiophene spacers (1.05 eq)  
- Warm slowly to –40°C, quench with degassed saturated NH₄Cl  
- Extract with DCM, dry over MgSO₄  
- Purification: Recrystallization from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 68–77%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Undecamethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + extra BHT-type radical scavenger (6 mol%)  
- Heat at 70–85°C for 4–7 h under dark conditions (monitor NIR shift)  
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE or diethyl ether  
- **Yield**: 64–75%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation & Crystallization
- React with 1,3-propane sultone (4 eq) in dry DMF at 60–70°C for 4–6 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid gradient)  
- Lyophilization followed by final recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain stable crystalline powder  
- **Recovery**: 80–88%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Storage
- **Analytical Data**: ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC purity ≥98%, ICP-MS for iodine content  
- UV-Vis-NIR spectrum (confirm deep NIR-IIb/III absorption and near-transparent visible profile)  
- Photoacoustic signal testing and photostability assay  
- **Storage Recommendation**: Amber vial at room temperature with silica gel desiccant. Protect from direct light.
