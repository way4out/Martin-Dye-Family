### Overview
Martinirubine-S is the **room-temperature shelf-stable** version of Martinirubine (MD-002). It extends the polymethine chain into the NIR-II window while incorporating antioxidant shielding and a crystalline zwitterionic form for easy storage without deep freezing. It maintains bright crimson color in concentrated solution that shifts toward near-clear when diluted.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Crimson-red in concentrated solution, shifts to near-clear when highly diluted
- **Absorption Range**: Strong NIR-II (1050–1150 nm) with excellent brightness and quantum yield
- **Photostability**: Excellent to near-infinite (rigidified structure + built-in hindered phenol antioxidants)
- **Solubility**: Excellent in water, ethanol, DMSO, lipids, and polymer matrices (sulfonated/PEG form)
- **Molecular Formula (proposed)**: C₄₂H₅₀N₂O₇S₂Na₂ (extended nonamethine shielded version)
- **Purity Target**: ≥98% (optical grade)
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE bottle at **room temperature (15–25°C)** for 12+ months. Refrigeration extends stability to 2+ years.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 62–80%  
**Purity**: ≥98% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Extended Core Assembly
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)  
- Appropriate secondary amines (2.2 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant precursor (0.6 eq)  
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2.5 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 16–20 h  
- Workup: Cool, Celite filtration, concentrate  
- Purification: Flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc gradient) + recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 74–84%

#### Step 2: Organolithium Extended Bridge Precursor
- Cool shielded core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi (2.2 eq) dropwise, stir 90–120 min  
- Add extended nonamethine linker precursor with thiophene spacers (1.05 eq)  
- Warm slowly to –40°C, quench with degassed saturated NH₄Cl  
- Extract with DCM, dry over MgSO₄  
- Purification: Recrystallization from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 70–79%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Nonamethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt linker (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + extra BHT-type radical scavenger (5 mol%)  
- Heat at 75–90°C for 4–6 h under dark conditions (monitor NIR shift)  
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE or diethyl ether  
- **Yield**: 65–78%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation & Crystallization
- React with 1,3-propane sultone (3.5 eq) in dry DMF at 60–70°C for 4–5 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeOH/H₂O + 0.1% TFA gradient)  
- Lyophilization followed by final recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain stable crystalline powder  
- **Recovery**: 80–88%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Storage
- **Analytical Data**: ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC purity ≥98%  
- UV-Vis-NIR spectrum (confirm strong NIR-II peak ~1100 nm and crimson-to-clear behavior)  
- Photostability test under continuous NIR illumination  
- **Storage Recommendation**: Amber vial at room temperature with silica gel desiccant packet. Protect from direct light and moisture.
