### Overview
AetherClear-4S is the **room-temperature shelf-stable** version of AetherClear-4. It maintains the signature near-colorless optical clarity (>90–95% visible transmittance) and ultra-stable NIR-II performance while adding enhanced antioxidant shielding, a more crystalline zwitterionic form, and improved resistance to oxidation and humidity for practical everyday storage and use.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Near-colorless / pale straw (>90–95% transmittance 400–700 nm)
- **Absorption Range**: Selective NIR-II enhancement (700–1100+ nm) with tuned hybrid structure
- **Photostability**: Extreme to near-infinite (antiaromatic self-healing + built-in hindered phenol antioxidants)
- **Solubility**: Universal — excellent in water, ethanol, DMSO, lipids, and polymer matrices
- **Molecular Formula (proposed)**: C₄₈H₆₂N₃O₁₂S₃Na₃ (hybrid aminofluorene shielded version)
- **Purity Target**: ≥99% (optical grade)
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE bottle at **room temperature (15–25°C)** for 12+ months. Refrigeration optional for 2+ years.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 65–82%  
**Purity**: ≥99% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Hybrid Aminofluorene Core
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)  
- PEG-amine mix (2.3 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant precursor (0.6 eq)  
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2.5 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 14–18 h  
- Workup: Celite filtration, concentrate  
- Purification: Flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc gradient) + recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 76–86%

#### Step 2: Stabilized Organolithium Bridge Precursor
- Cool shielded core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi (2.2 eq) dropwise, stir 90–120 min  
- Add rigidified heptamethine/nonamethine hybrid linker with thiophene spacers (1.05 eq)  
- Warm slowly to –40°C, quench with degassed saturated NH₄Cl  
- Extract with DCM, dry over MgSO₄  
- Purification: Recrystallization from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 72–81%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Polymethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + extra BHT-type radical scavenger (5 mol%)  
- Heat at 75–90°C for 3–6 h under dark conditions (monitor NIR shift)  
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE or diethyl ether  
- **Yield**: 68–78%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation & Crystallization
- React with 1,3-propane sultone (3.5 eq) in dry DMF at 60–70°C for 4 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid gradient)  
- Lyophilization followed by final recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain highly stable crystalline powder  
- **Recovery**: 85–92%
