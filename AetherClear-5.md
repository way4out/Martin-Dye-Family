Overview
AetherVoid-5 is the ultimate evolution and current flagship of the Martin Dye Family. It surpasses all previous members (Martinicyanine A, Martinirubine, Martinivoid-Ph, and AetherClear-4) in every performance metric: maximum visible transparency, broadest/deepest SWIR absorption, near-infinite photostability, and easiest synthesis.

Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol (Self-Contained)
Target Scale: 200–500 mg purified product
Expected Overall Yield: 65–85%
Purity: ≥99% (HPLC)
Reagents Overview (for ~500 mg scale)
•  Advanced 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative
•  PEG-amine mix + cyclopentadiene precursor
•  Rigid dodecamethine linker
•  Pd-PEPPSI-IPr catalyst
•  t-BuLi, piperidine, BHT scavenger
•  1,3-propane sultone
•  HPLC solvents (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid)
All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.
Step 1: Super-Donor Core Assembly
•  Buchwald-Hartwig amination: 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq) + PEG-amine mix (2.3 eq) + cyclopentadiene precursor (1.1 eq)
•  Catalyst: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 14–20 h
•  Workup: Celite filtration, flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc), recrystallization from ethanol
•  Yield: 80–88%
Step 2: Organolithium Bridge Precursor
•  Cool core to –78°C in dry THF
•  Add t-BuLi (2.2 eq) dropwise, stir 90 min
•  Add rigid dodecamethine linker (1.05 eq)
•  Warm to –40°C, quench with degassed NH₄Cl
•  Extract (DCM), recrystallize (THF/hexane)
•  Yield: 75–82%
Step 3: Adaptive Polymethine Condensation
•  Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)
•  Add piperidine (10 mol%) + BHT scavenger
•  Heat 75–90°C for 3–6 h (dark)
•  Cool, triple precipitate with cold MTBE/diethyl ether
•  Yield: 70–80%
Step 4: Final Solubilization & Void Tuning
•  Sulfonation: 1,3-propane sultone (3–4 eq) in dry DMF, 65°C, 4 h
•  Counterion exchange to Na⁺
•  Final purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O gradient) + lyophilization
•  Optional recrystallization from ethanol/water for optical-grade crystals
•  Recovery: 85–92%
Step 5: Characterization
•  ¹H/¹³C NMR, HRMS
•  HPLC purity ≥99%
•  UV-Vis-NIR (flat visible baseline, confirm SWIR peaks)
•  Photostability assay under continuous NIR irradiation
