# AetherVoid-5 (MD-005)

**Superior Flagship Dye — Martin Dye Family Series #5**

### Core Innovation
AetherVoid-5 achieves **true optical void** behavior: >99% transmittance across the entire visible spectrum while delivering record-breaking SWIR absorption and self-regenerating photostability through a fused antiaromatic super-donor core + adaptive dodecamethine bridge.

### Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Water-clear / optically void (>99% transmittance 400–700 nm)
- **Absorption**: Ultra-broad SWIR (strong dual peaks ~950 nm and ~1650 nm, massive tail >2200 nm)
- **Photostability**: Near-infinite (antiaromatic reversal + radical-scavenging cage + auto-regenerating conjugation)
- **Solubility**: Universal (water, ethanol, DMSO, lipids, polymers)
- **Purity Target**: ≥99% (optical grade)
- **Molecular Formula** (proposed): C₆₂H₇₈N₄O₁₂S₃Na₃ (example; exact in SMILES)
- **Key Applications**: Invisible IR filters, deep-tissue theranostics, night-vision overlays, quantum sensing, bio-implants, spray-on SWIR coatings

### Optimized Synthesis Protocol (Highest Yield & Purity)
(See full step-by-step below or SYNTHESIS.md for complete reagent tables)

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 65–85%  
**Purity**: ≥99% after final HPLC

#### Step 1: Super-Donor Core (Fused Aminofluorene-Cyclopentadiene)
- Buchwald-Hartwig with Pd-PEPPSI-IPr catalyst, 105–115°C, 14–20 h  
- Yield: 80–88%

#### Step 2: Organolithium Bridge Precursor
- t-BuLi at –78°C, rigid dodecamethine linker  
- Yield: 75–82%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Polymethine Condensation
- Pyridine/Ac₂O, piperidine + BHT scavenger, 75–90°C, 3–6 h  
- Triple precipitation  
- Yield: 70–80%

#### Step 4: Final Solubilization & Tuning
- Sulfonation + PEG extension  
- C18 HPLC + lyophilization  
- Yield: 85–92%

#### Step 5: Characterization
- NMR, HRMS, HPLC, UV-Vis-NIR, photostability assay
