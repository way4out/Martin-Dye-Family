### Overview
AetherGenesis-7 is the most advanced member of the Martin Series. It is a single fixed supramolecular hybrid cyanine platform designed to outperform Tucyanin-F(S) in visible transparency, photostability, and practical usability while remaining fully shelf-stable and low-toxicity in final applied forms.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Near-perfect optically void (>99.8–99.9% transmittance 400–700 nm)
- **Absorption Range**: Ultra-broad SWIR (950–2500+ nm) with strong, usable peaks and adaptive tail
- **Photostability**: Superior (rigidification + supramolecular self-repair mechanisms)
- **Shelf Stability**: Room temperature (15–25°C) stable for 24–48+ months in crystalline form
- **Toxicity Profile**: Low in final applied forms (biocompatible PEG/sulfonate shielding)
- **Purity Target**: ≥99% (optical grade)

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 52–72%  
**Purity**: ≥99% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion, and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Anti-Aromatic Core Assembly
- 2,7-dibromo-9-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)
- PEG-amine mix (2.4 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant (0.7 eq) + cyclopentadiene quinoidal precursor (1.1 eq)
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2.8 mol%), NaOtBu (3.8 eq), dry toluene, 110–118°C, 18–24 h
- Workup: Celite filtration, flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc), recrystallization from ethanol
- **Yield**: 72–82%

#### Step 2: Extended Adaptive Bridge Construction
- Cool core to –78°C in dry THF
- Add t-BuLi (2.4 eq) dropwise, stir 120–150 min
- Add rigid dodecamethine linker with embedded thiophene spacers and hydrogen-bonding sites (1.06 eq)
- Quench with degassed NH₄Cl, extract DCM, recrystallize THF/hexane
- **Yield**: 68–78%

#### Step 3: Supramolecular Condensation & Tuning
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)
- Add piperidine (12 mol%) + dual radical scavengers (7 mol%)
- Heat 70–86°C for 4–6 h under dark conditions
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE
- **Yield**: 62–74%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation, Shielding & Crystallization
- 1,3-propane sultone (4.2 eq) in dry DMF, 58–68°C, 4–6 h
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form
- Purification: Multiple passes of reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O + 0.05% formic acid)
- Lyophilization followed by repeated recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain highly stable crystalline powder
- **Recovery**: 82–90%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Storage
- ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC purity ≥99%
- UV-Vis-NIR (confirm high visible transparency + broad SWIR response)
- Photostability and shelf-life testing
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE container at room temperature with desiccant. Highly stable.
