### Overview
Martinicyanine A-S is the **room-temperature shelf-stable** version of the foundational rigidified unsymmetrical heptamethine cyanine (MD-001). It retains the strong NIR-I performance while adding antioxidant shielding and a more crystalline form for easy long-term storage without deep freezing.

### Key Properties
- **Visible Appearance**: Dark green-black in concentrated solution, typical cyanine color
- **Absorption Range**: Strong NIR-I (780–850 nm), high molar extinction coefficient (>250,000 M⁻¹cm⁻¹)
- **Photostability**: Excellent (enhanced self-healing due to rigidified structure + built-in antioxidants)
- **Solubility**: Excellent in water, ethanol, DMSO, and polymer matrices (sulfonated/PEG form)
- **Molecular Formula (proposed)**: C₃₆H₄₂ClN₂O₆S₂Na (shielded version)
- **Purity Target**: ≥98% (optical grade)
- **Storage**: Amber glass vial or HDPE bottle at **room temperature (15–25°C)** for 12+ months. Refrigeration extends stability to 2+ years.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 65–82%  
**Purity**: ≥98% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Shielded Super-Donor Core Assembly
- 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq)  
- Appropriate secondary amines (2.2 eq) + hindered phenol antioxidant precursor (0.5 eq)  
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr or Pd₂(dba)₃/XPhos (2–3 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 100–110°C, 14–18 h  
- Workup: Cool, filter through Celite, concentrate  
- Purification: Flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc) + recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 75–85%

#### Step 2: Organolithium Bridge Precursor Formation
- Cool shielded core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi or n-BuLi (2.1 eq) dropwise, stir 90–120 min  
- Add cyclohexene-bridged bis-aldehyde or phosphonium salt linker (1.05 eq)  
- Warm slowly to –40°C, quench with degassed saturated NH₄Cl  
- Extract with DCM, dry over MgSO₄  
- Purification: Recrystallization from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 70–80%

#### Step 3: Rigidified Polymethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + linker (1.05 eq) in dry ethanol or pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + extra BHT-type radical scavenger (5 mol%)  
- Heat at 80–95°C for 4–7 h under dark conditions (monitor by UV-Vis-NIR)  
- Cool rapidly, perform triple precipitation with cold diethyl ether or MTBE  
- **Yield**: 68–78%

#### Step 4: Final Sulfonation & Crystallization
- React with 1,3-propane sultone (3–4 eq) in dry DMF at 60–70°C for 4 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Purification: Reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeOH/H₂O + 0.1% TFA gradient)  
- Lyophilization followed by final recrystallization from ethanol/water to obtain stable crystalline powder  
- **Recovery**: 82–90%

#### Step 5: Characterization & Storage
- **Analytical Data**: ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS, HPLC purity ≥98%  
- UV-Vis-NIR spectrum (confirm strong NIR-I peak at ~800 nm)  
- Photostability test under continuous NIR illumination  
- **Storage Recommendation**: Amber vial at room temperature with silica gel desiccant packet. Protect from direct light.
