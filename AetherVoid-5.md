### Overview

It surpasses all previous members (Martinicyanine A, Martinirubine, Martinivoid-Ph, and AetherClear-4) in every performance metric: maximum visible transparency, broadest and deepest SWIR absorption, near-infinite photostability, and streamlined synthesis.

### Complete Optimized Synthesis Protocol

**Target Scale**: 200–500 mg purified product  
**Expected Overall Yield**: 65–85%  
**Purity**: ≥99% (HPLC)

**All steps performed under strict anhydrous Ar/N₂ atmosphere, full light exclusion (foil-wrapped glassware), and fume hood.**

#### Step 1: Super-Donor Core Assembly
- Buchwald-Hartwig amination using 2,7-dibromo-fluorenone derivative (1.0 eq) + PEG-amine mix (2.3 eq) + cyclopentadiene precursor (1.1 eq)  
- Catalyst: Pd-PEPPSI-IPr (2 mol%), NaOtBu (3.5 eq), dry toluene, 105–115°C, 14–20 h  
- Workup: Celite filtration, flash chromatography (hexane/EtOAc gradient), recrystallization from ethanol  
- **Yield**: 80–88%

#### Step 2: Organolithium Bridge Precursor
- Cool core to –78°C in dry THF  
- Add t-BuLi (2.2 eq) dropwise, stir 90 min  
- Add rigid dodecamethine linker (1.05 eq)  
- Warm to –40°C, quench with degassed NH₄Cl solution  
- Extract with DCM, recrystallize from THF/hexane  
- **Yield**: 75–82%

#### Step 3: Adaptive Polymethine Condensation
- Intermediate (1 eq) + bis-aldehyde/phosphonium salt (1.08 eq) in pyridine/Ac₂O (1:1)  
- Add piperidine (10 mol%) + BHT radical scavenger  
- Heat at 75–90°C for 3–6 h under dark conditions  
- Cool rapidly and perform triple precipitation with cold MTBE or diethyl ether  
- **Yield**: 70–80%

#### Step 4: Final Solubilization & Void Tuning
- Sulfonation with 1,3-propane sultone (3–4 eq) in dry DMF at 65°C for 4 h  
- Counterion exchange to Na⁺ form  
- Final purification by reverse-phase C18 HPLC (MeCN/H₂O gradient with 0.05% formic acid) followed by lyophilization  
- Optional recrystallization from ethanol/water for optical-grade material  
- **Recovery**: 85–92%

#### Step 5: Characterization
- ¹H NMR, ¹³C NMR, HRMS  
- HPLC purity ≥99%  
- UV-Vis-NIR spectrum (confirm flat visible baseline and SWIR peaks)  
- Photostability testing under continuous NIR irradiation
